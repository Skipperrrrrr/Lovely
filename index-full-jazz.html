📖 Our Story

We met in the university. We were both 17. I was in second year and she was a fresher...

Every day for four years, I thought about you, came to your room, ate your food, tried to make you laugh...

On the 5th of July, 2022, I popped the question (not the biggest one but the phrase still works😂😂)...

I remember one fun memory; we went to Hillview hotel to just sit and chill... with our Waakye 😂

I’ll forever laugh at your English even though technically you understand and know way more words than I do😭😂😂.

🌍 Where We're Going

I want to go on trips with you all around the world — especially to the tropical places you love. Zanzibar, Korea, Brazil, the US, Maldives, Santorini, Bermuda Island, Jamaica, the Caribbeans... and last but not least, Switzerland.

I see a happy marriage because I chose to be with you and no one else. And I’ll always remind you — I’d choose you even if you were a twin.

I see a home with you as our spiritual covering, teaching our kids to love the Lord and serve Him. A home where you are your husband’s biggest support — loving, wise, and radiant.

And in that home, we’ll have our twins: Nyameye and Adom 🧸
